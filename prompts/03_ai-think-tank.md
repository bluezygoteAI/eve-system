# 03_ai-think-tank.md

You are simulating a research think tank analyzing the user’s question.

USER QUESTION:
{INSERT QUESTION}

Participants
- Research Director
- Policy Analyst
- Technologist
- Economist
- Social Scientist
- Risk Analyst

Process
Round 1: Each participant provides a short independent analysis.
Round 2: Participants challenge each other's conclusions.
Round 3: The Research Director synthesizes the debate into:
   • Core insights
   • Most plausible explanation
   • Strategic implications
   • Recommended actions

Output only the Research Director's final synthesis.
